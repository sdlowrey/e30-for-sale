Overview

Based on a 1987 BMW 325is, this car was tracked as a street car with various clubs before being prepped for SCCA E Production racing in 2009. The car was entered mostly in SCCA races and PDX events in the Mid-Atlantic area. Achieved several class wins and a season championship. Last event was October 2013.

This is a solid, reliable car that's been stored in a climate-controlled garage. It starts and runs fine. Will need new tires and tech inspection. Could use some cosmetic touch-ups too. Otherwise, it's ready to go.

Has SCCA logbook and documentation/paperwork for pretty much everything that I've worked on.

Engine & Drivetrain

BMW M20 with stock internals. Mileage unknown, rockers replaced in 2009.

  • Motronic 172 DME (have original 152 DME)
  • Ireland Engineering shorty header
  • Custom side-exit exhaust, 18" glass pack race muffler
  • I-J crank scraper
  • Powermaster Denso-style race alternator
  • Spal 16" electric puller fan
  • Lightweight aluminum flywheel
  • Stripped engine wiring harness

The wiring harness was stripped to remove OBD1, fuel and O2 heat relays, gauge cluster, etc.

All unnecessary coolant lines removed. No thermostat. Power steering pump removed; can be reinstalled and reconnected to stock steering rack.

Red Line 50W racing oil for the motor, distilled water for cooling.

Stock saddle tank removed, FuelSafe racing cell mounted flush with trunk floor (spare tire well removed). Holley 8psi first-stage pump, Kinsler swirl pot, metered return line, high-pressure second stage pump and filter. Interior send and return lines with fittings at the rear bulkhead and firewall. Stock fuel rail, regulator, and injectors. Electric fuel pressure gauge on the dash. Runs on pump gas.

Five-speed Getrag 265, ClutchMasters Stage 5 ceramic clutch, B&M short shifter. Stock drive shaft and center bearing, 4.10 rear diff, stock axles and hubs. Solid Delrin diff mount, urethane engine and gearbox mounts. Red Line fluids.

Suspension, Brakes, Wheels

Ground Control Systems fully adjustable race suspension. Currently set up for Hoosier 245/580R15 competition radial slicks.

  • Koni single-adjustable race dampers
  • Ireland Engineering front and rear sway bars
  • Shortened and gussetted front struts with M3-style sway bar links
  • Eibach 2.5" coilover springs; 550 lb/in front, 850 rear
  • Adjustable height spring perches
  • Racing camber/caster plates
  • Urethane rear upper shock mounts
  • Ireland Engineering adjustable rear camber/toe kit
  • Spherical front control arm bearings
  • Reinforced front subframe and rear trailing arms
  • Solid aluminum rear subframe mount

D-Force 15" wheels, bullet-nose studs, Turner spacers. Also have a set of Team Dynamics wheels that I use for mounting rain tires.

Brakes use stock rotors and calipers with stainless steel caliper lines and brass bushings. I used Hawk DTC-60 pads on the front and HT-10's on the rear. Brake fluid is ATE Blue.

ABS module deleted. New brake lines. Front-rear brake bias control mounted on tunnel next to driver seat. Emergency brake deleted.

Chassis

Ireland Engineering fiberglass hood (5 pins) and deck lid (Dzus fastened). Lexan rear and quarter windows. Rear bumper delete. Sunroof delete. Ireland Engineering ABS plastic fender flares.

The hood has a small crack from a minor bounce off a tire wall at Summit Point (oil on the track in a braking zone - yahoo!). The right front fender was replaced as a result (it's flat black and doesn't match the stock gloss black paint.) The damage was cosmetic; I raced the car later the same day without any problems.

Roll cage built by Piper Motorsport in Virginia. Anchored to firewall and trunk floor. Includes door bars on the driver side, dead pedal, and window net attachment points. SCCA cage #42-3718.

Original dash gutted, cluster deleted, custom fuse box on tunnel. Optima gel battery mounted on the floor.

Wink 4-panel rear view mirror, stock side mirrors. Sparco Pro ADV racing seat (expired?). Schroth 6-point harness anchored ro floor and cage. No passenger seat.
